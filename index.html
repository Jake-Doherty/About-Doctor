All About The Doctor

My cat Doctor has been a welcome companion for the last 9 years, and soon celebrates his 10th birthday in October! He is a black and white piebald with a very fetching 'Phantom of the Opera' mask. Doctor has a brother named "Chicken Man" and a sister named "Penny". They tolerate each other. Doctor has lived his life indoors for the most part, but enjoyed a brief period as a farm cat while I lived with my parents.

  • He LOVES his catnip first thing in the morning
  • He was rescued when he was only about 4 weeks old
  • He is very fond of ear rubs, and will thank you kindly for them.
